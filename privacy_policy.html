Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

I (“Pomyx”, “I”, “me”) respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use my applications and services. This application is an icon pack and does not provide user accounts, online services, or cloud features. Optional in-app purchases may be offered solely to support development.

1. Information Collection

My icon pack applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. I do not require user accounts and I do not request names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal information. Payment-related information may be processed by Google when making in-app purchases, in accordance with Google’s own privacy policies. I do not have access to this information.

2. Automatically Collected Data

My applications do not use analytics, tracking systems, advertising frameworks, or profiling tools. I do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data.

3. Permissions

My applications request only permissions necessary for basic app functionality and compatibility with Android launchers and system features. No permissions are used to access personal data such as location, contacts, microphone, camera, or messages.

4. Third-Party Services

My applications do not integrate advertising networks or analytics services. If the app offers optional in-app purchases (donations), those payments are handled by Google Play Billing and are subject to Google’s own privacy policies. If I provide external links (such as GitHub or support pages), those services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Donations / in-app purchases (Google Play Billing)

This app may offer optional one-time purchases (donations) to support development. Payments are processed securely by Google Play Billing. I do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information (such as credit card details). Google may process certain information required to complete the transaction (e.g., billing and tax information) in accordance with Google’s policies.

6. Data Security

Since I do not collect or store personal data, there is no personal data stored by me to secure within the apps.

7. Children’s Privacy

My applications are suitable for all ages and I do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be published on this page.
